About Farmering :

Farming is the act or process of working the ground, planting seeds, and growing edible plants. You can also describe raising animals for milk or meat as farming.

Farmers grow crops and move to other lands when old fertility diminishes. Benefits include pest control and maximum yield.
Intensive Farming
Small farms are cultivated using large inputs of manual labor, fertilizer, and manure. Benefits include increased productivity and multiple crop cultivation.
